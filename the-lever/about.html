We got tired of the same advice recycled in different fonts.

The short version

Kyle and Reese work inside local businesses — restaurants, salons, clinics, trades. Every week they test AI tools, automation workflows, and marketing strategies. Not in a lab. Not in a case study from 2019. Inside real businesses with real budgets and real customers who just want their food hot and their haircut right.

They got frustrated. About 90% of marketing advice on the internet is written by people who have never run a Google Ads campaign for a burger restaurant. Who’ve never debugged a booking system for a salon at 11pm on a Tuesday. Who talk about “leveraging AI” but couldn’t tell you what happens when you actually try it on a real business with a real budget.

So they started writing about what actually works. Based on what they actually test. Every Friday, one email. No sponsors, no affiliate links, no “here’s my course.” Just: here’s what we tried this week, here’s what happened, here’s the number.

That’s The Lever. A newsletter for people who run local businesses and are tired of guessing.
