How this site was made

Design Philosophy

This site was built to last 20 years. No frameworks, no build tools, no npm install. Pure HTML, CSS, and a few lines of JavaScript. If the internet still exists in 2046, this site will still work exactly as it does today.

Every decision was made with longevity in mind. No dependencies to break, no packages to update, no services to sunset. Just files on a server, doing what files on a server have always done.

Typography

Fraunces for headlines — a variable font with optical sizing that gives our headings their warm, slightly quirky character. It feels handmade without being precious about it.

Outfit for body text — clean, geometric, reads beautifully at every size. Both are self-hosted. No CDN dependency. If Google Fonts goes down tomorrow, you'd never know.

The Name

A lever is the simplest machine. Small effort, big result. That's what we want this newsletter to be for local businesses — the small, specific insight that moves the whole thing.

Also, we liked how it looked as a logo. Sometimes it's that simple.
